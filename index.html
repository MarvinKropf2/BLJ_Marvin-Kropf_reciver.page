Vanilla Crescents

    Ingredients for 8 people:

  • 250 g Flour
  • 100 g Peeled ground almonds
  • 80 g Icing sugar
  • 1 packet Vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch Salt
  • 200 g Cold butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 6 tbsp Icing sugar
  • 1 packet Vanilla sugar

Preparation:

  • Prepare ingredients: Put flour, ground almonds, icing sugar, vanilla sugar, and a pinch of salt in a bowl and mix thoroughly.

  • Prepare butter: Cut the butter into small pieces and add it to the dry ingredients in the bowl. Rub the butter into the dry ingredients using your fingers until a crumbly mixture forms.

  • Prepare egg: Whisk the egg in a separate bowl.

  • Make dough: Add the whisked egg to the crumbly mixture in the bowl. Combine everything to form a dough by mixing the ingredients well.

  • Let dough rest: Shape the dough into a flat disc and wrap it in cling film or cover it in a bowl. Let it rest in the refrigerator for about 2 hours.

  • Preheat oven: Preheat the oven to 160 degrees Celsius.

  • Shape dough: Take the chilled dough in portions and shape it into finger-thick rolls. Cut these rolls into about 2 cm long pieces, then twist them into pencil-thick crescents.

  • Cool before baking: Place the shaped crescents on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and chill in the refrigerator for about 15 minutes to firm up.

  • Bake: Bake the chilled crescents in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes in the middle of the oven until they turn golden brown. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly.

  • Decorate and cool: Mix icing sugar and vanilla sugar. Dust the still-warm vanilla crescents with the sugar mixture, carefully remove them from the parchment paper, and let them cool completely on a wire rack.

Additional Information

  • Makes approximately 50 pieces
  • Work with the dough only in portions and keep the rest in the refrigerator. When the dough is cold, it's easier to shape.
  • Shelf life: Store in a well-sealed container for about 2 weeks.
